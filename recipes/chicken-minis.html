Chicken Minis

Description

this recipe is based off the chick-fil-a chicken recipes. Not the exact recipe because i dont know how they actually make them. this also goes really good with honey glazed rolls

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders 1LB cube
  • 2 TBSP of mustard or to taste
  • 2 TSP of creole
  • 1 cups flour
  • 1/2 TBSP creole
  • 1 TSP Black Pepper
  • 1/2 TSP White Pepper
  • 2 TSP Onion powder
  • 1 TSP paprika
  • 1 TBSP sugar
  • Oil

Follow-Step-by-Step

  1. cut chicken in cubes and put in a bowel
  2. put mustard and 2TSP of creole with chicken and mix all togther
  3. let marinatie for 30 mins
  4. put the rest of the ingredients(besides oil) in a bowel and mix.(this is for seasoning)
  5. once done marinating, individually place one in seasoning and then on to a plate
  6. fill up a pan or pot with just enough oil to fully submerge a chicken cube
  7. once they start floating or look golden-brown place them on a plate to cool
  8. then serve
