Bacon and Egg Tacos

Description

this is a recipe that everyone should know and a recipe that everyone will like.
Instead of going to taco bell just make your own at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of bacon
  • 7 eggs
  • tortillas

Follow-Step-by-Step

  1. cut bacon in slices
  2. cook for 10 - 15 or until it starts to look a little crispy
  3. while the bacon cooks, crack 7 eggs into a bowel and whisk togther
  4. when bacon is done, throwout the grease out of pan
  5. pour eggs in with the bacon
  6. mix occasionally until eggs are done
