Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A good snack for before bed, afterbed, in bed, or whenever. This is a simple recipe that hardly requires any work

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Cups Dark Chocolate Chips
  • 1 TBSP vaillna Extract
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Cup light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup or 2 Sticks of Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 TSP Salt
  • 1/2 Baking Powder
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 1 TSP of Baking Soda

Follow-Step-by-Step

  1. Mix Dry indegredients in a bowel and set aside
  2. creame butter and sugars togther in seperate bowel
  3. add eggs and vannila into that mixture and mix into fluffy
  4. add dry to wet indegredients and mix togther
  5. fold in chocolate chips
  6. bake at 375F for 9-10 mins on a parchment paper lined baking sheet
  7. let cool. then serve.
